My Favourite Albums

These are some but not all of my favourite albums of all time.

Frank Ocean - Blond

Though it's released in 2016, I wasn't paying much attention to Frank Ocean untill about one or two years after when Hip-Hop and R&B slowly grew on me. And it is definitely one of my favourite albums considering its unique vibe that is kind of different with the R&B I have heard of. Also, Frank Ocean's charisma is so irreplacable.

Flume - Flume

It was released in 2012 while I was a freshman in the university. Despite not as big as it was, EDM was still a very popular genre. However, the sound was pretty boring to me but I still found my spot on the electronic category, which is downtempo. Flume, Chet Baker, and those other acts in the Future Classic, an Australian label, were among those I was very inspired by at the time. But honestly speaking, I really hate their later albums.

As I Lay Dying - An Ocean Between Us

It was released in 2007. I discovered this album when I was in secondary school. That was around 2012 when I was still a metalhead and also listened to lots of rock and punk music. The first time I watched the video of The Sound of Truth on YouTube, I was so amazed and decided to form a metalcore band and I did.

God Is An Astronaut – All Is Violent, All Is Bright

If I have to name one single band that musically inspired me the most. That is God Is An Astronaut. And my favourite album of theirs is this one, released in 2005. I love everything about this band and I formed a Post-Rock band because of them.
